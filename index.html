I am a PhD student in analytic number theory at Chalmers University of Technology, advised by Julia Brandes and Simon Rydin Myerson. Before moving to Gothenburg, I got a master's degree in Mathematics from ETH Zürich, where I wrote my master's thesis on Zaremba's conjecture under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Claire Burrin. I have a bachelor's degree from the University of Edinburgh.

Research

My research focuses on Diophantine equations and the Hardy-Littlewoord circle method. My recent works explore:

  • Diagonal forms on hypersurfaces (arXiv, ELAZ 2026)
  • The Hasse principle for n-ary forms on hypersurfaces
